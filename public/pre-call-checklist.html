Pre-Call Checklist

Complete 5 minutes before call — prevents 90% of fumbles

1. Environment (T-5 min)

Camera framing
Eye level, chest-up, neutral background. Light from front, not behind.
Audio check
Test mic. Close other audio apps. Headphones preferred.
Notifications OFF
Slack, email, calendar, system notifications — all silenced.

2. Screen Share Setup (T-3 min)

Browser tabs (left → right)
Only these 4 tabs, in this order:
  • asadi.ai (hero visible)
  • asadi.ai/#work (products section)
  • asadi.ai/#evidence (Evidence section)
  • cal.com/ctoalpha/deep-dive (Strategy Consult)
Close everything else
  • Slack / Discord
  • Email client
  • IDE / Terminal
  • Any other browser windows

    • 3. Product Selection (before call)

    Pick primary product based on their context

    Platform / Orchestration

    EdTech / Marketplace
    Finura
    Know the Outcome line
    Read the "Outcome:" text on the product card before the call.

    4. Materials Ready

    Caller Notes visible
    Print or second screen: asadi.ai/call-notes.html
    Notepad ready
    For capturing their answers to the 3 discovery questions.

    5. Final Check (T-1 min)

    Browser zoom at 100%
    Cmd/Ctrl + 0 to reset.
    Water nearby
    Phone on silent, face-down
