10-Minute Call

Caller Notes — Print & Keep Visible

0:00 Frame + Agenda
"I'll do this in 10 minutes: 60 seconds context, 6 minutes proof in the UI, then 3 minutes on your constraints and next steps."

→ Camera on, still posture, then share screen.

0:45 The Hook
"I ship at startup speed with enterprise-grade auditability. The reason that matters is: speed without governance creates risk. My work is built around receipts—logs, policies, and deterministic behavior."

→ Pause 2 seconds. Screen: portfolio hero only.

1:45 Proof #1 — One Product
"This is [Product]. The outcome is [Outcome line]. The proof is in the audit trail and idempotent workflows—every decision produces a receipt."

→ Open product card. Point to ONE receipt element. Stop talking while you zoom.

4:30 Proof #2 — Pattern Travels
"Different domain, same spine: tenant-safe defaults, traceability, and fail-closed behavior."
"That's why these ship in weeks—reuse the governed backbone, swap the domain modules."

→ Show second product (30–45 sec max).

6:30 Discovery — 3 Questions Only
  1. "What's the highest-cost failure mode you're trying to avoid?"
  2. "What's your data residency / security boundary?"
  3. "What decision needs to become faster or safer in the next 30–60 days?"

→ Authority Pause on interruptions: stop, nod once, 1-sec delay, then answer.

8:30 The Close

If fit is strong:

"Next step: a 30-minute strategy consult where we map your constraints → a scoped proof. I'll leave with a 1-page plan and measurable success criteria."

If fit is uncertain:

"Next step: a 15-minute fit check to confirm scope and constraints. If it's viable, we book the strategy consult."
9:30 Exit Line
"Before we end—what would make this a 'yes' internally? Security sign-off, ROI, or integration feasibility?"
"Perfect. I'll follow up with the exact artifacts you need."
